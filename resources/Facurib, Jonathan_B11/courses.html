Contact Center Services NC II

Requirements

1. Applicant must pass the Pre-Assessment Exam.

2. Applicant must be 18 years old and above.

3. Applicant must be unemployed during the application.

4. Applicant must be at least a Senior High School graduate.

5. Applicant must be a resident of Negros Occidental.

6. Applicant must be of good moral character and in good health.

7. Applicant must belong to a family whose socio-economic information meet the cut-off indicators.

8. Applicant must be able to communicate in English both oral and written.

2. Applicant must be 18 years old and above.

3. Applicant must be unemployed during the application.

4. Applicant must be at least a Senior High School graduate.

5. Applicant must be a resident of Negros Occidental.
